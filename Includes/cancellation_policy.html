No cancellation Policy

At Posh.com, we strive to provide you with the best possible experience. We understand that sometimes circumstances change, and you may need to modify or cancel an order placed on Posh.com However, we would like to inform you that we have implemented a strict No-cancellation Policy to ensure that we can provide you with the highest level of service and satisfaction. We understand that unforeseen circumstances can arise that may necessitate a cancellation or modification of an order, but we ask for your cooperation and understanding of our No-cancellation Policy.

1.

NO CANCELLATION

Once an order has been placed and payment has been processed, it cannot be cancelled or modified. This Policy is in place to ensure that our team can efficiently process orders and fulfill them in a timely manner. Additionally, cancellations can cause delays in the processing and shipping of orders, which can negatively impact our customers.

2.

REVIEW YOUR ORDER

To avoid any issues with your order, we highly recommend that you carefully review your order before submitting it to ensure that it is accurate and complete. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please do not hesitate to contact our customer service team at +91 98765 43210 or Posh.com Our team will be happy to assist you in any way possible.

3.

OTHER SITUATIONS

If an item you have ordered is out of stock, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer you the option to either wait for the item to become available or cancel the order. However, once the item has been shipped, we cannot cancel the order.

In the event of a mistake on our part, such as an incorrect item being shipped or a defective product being received, we will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly and efficiently as possible. This may include providing a replacement item, issuing a refund, or offering store credit.

4.

CONTACT US

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation with this Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us. We thank you for choosing Posh.com and look forward to serving you.
